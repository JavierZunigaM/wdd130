Family Float Adventure

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Easy

Perfect for families with young children! This gentle float trip takes you through scenic canyons with calm waters and occasional small rapids. Enjoy wildlife viewing and swimming opportunities along the way. Our guides share fascinating stories about the area's history and ecology.

Price: $75 per person | Min Age: 5 years

Half Day Rapids Challenge

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Moderate

Experience the thrill of Class III rapids on this exciting half-day adventure! Navigate through exciting whitewater sections while enjoying stunning mountain views. Includes a riverside lunch and swimming break. Great for first-time rafters looking for excitement.

Price: $125 per person | Min Age: 12 years

Full Day Extreme Expedition

Duration: 8 hours | Difficulty: Advanced

Our most challenging adventure! Tackle Class IV and V rapids on this full-day expedition through remote wilderness. This trip is for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush. Includes gourmet riverside lunch, professional photos, and celebration at the end.

Price: $225 per person | Min Age: 16 years
