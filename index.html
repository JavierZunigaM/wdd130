San Jose - Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a tropical country in Central America, with a population of approximately 5 million people. It is bordered by Nicaragua to the north, Panama to the south, the Caribbean Sea to the northeast, and the Pacific Ocean to the southwest. The country has two main seasons, a dry season (December to April) and a rainy season (May to November), although the climate varies greatly by altitude and proximity to the oceans. Its highlights include its great biodiversity, more than 25% of its territory protected in national parks, and its tropical climate that ranges from temperate and cloudy in the highlands to warm and humid on the coasts. Costa Ricans call themselves "Ticos" (males) or "Ticas" (females). Also a unique word that describes Costa Ricans is, "Pura Vida".
